Related Projects

StreetBite Asian

StreetBite Asian is a regional food discovery platform focused on East and Southeast Asian countries. It highlights street food vendors, travel spots, and culinary stories across cities like Bangkok, Jakarta, Taipei, and more.

Street2025

Street2025 is a forward-looking initiative exploring the future of street food in urban environments. It supports digital innovation for local vendors and envisions smart street food zones in smart cities by the year 2025.
