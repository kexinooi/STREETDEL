Features of StreetDeli

All Food

StreetDeli provides a wide catalog of street food options. Users can browse by category, region, popularity, or new uploads. Whether you're looking for noodles, skewers, desserts, or drinks — it's all here at your fingertips.

Blog & Article

Our blog brings stories behind the food: from vendor interviews and local food festivals to cultural food histories. Stay updated with our latest features, guides, and tips on street food adventures across Malaysia.
