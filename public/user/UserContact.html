Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my profile information?
Go to the Settings section from the dashboard menu. Here you can edit your name, email, password, and preferences.
Is there a way to order food through Street Food Explorer?
Currently, Street Food Explorer helps you discover and explore food stalls. Some stalls may provide links to delivery partners (GrabFood, FoodPanda, etc.) if available.
Do I need internet access to use the app?
Yes, internet access is required to load maps, stall details, and user reviews.
Can I use Street Food Explorer in different cities?
Yes! You can explore stalls in multiple cities by searching or selecting your preferred location from the dashboard.
